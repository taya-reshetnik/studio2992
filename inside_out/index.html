Inside/Out

'Inside/Out' is a series of works for the exhibition 'Sexy Times' which includes a booklet, poem and an installation. The booklet is an associative collage, forming a narrative about the inner egoistic self and the outer other, about the relationship between the two. The interactive installation is a continuation of the same narrative, brought into the physical three dimensional-space. In order to activate the installation two individuals have to make a physical contact.
