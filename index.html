Multidisciplinary graphic design and research studio of Taya Reshetnik based in The Hague, Netherlands. studio2992's autonomous work emerges from investigative and microhistorical research and tells visual stories about human experience in urban environment. Among commissioned and collaborative projects are examples of graphics and navigation designs for exhibitions, visual identities, social media campaigns, web and print designs for museums, cultural events and artist initiatives.
