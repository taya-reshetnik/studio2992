Gesture

'Anthropometry: The Ornament of Gesture' is a graduation project of multidisciplinary designer Petra Popangelova. For her work at the department of Interior Architecture and Furniture Design at KABK, she researched the history of the craft of spatial ornaments and its exceeding relation to human gestures in modern times. 'Gesture Legend' is a complementary booklet with infographics, explaining all the gestures used for creation of the installation. The leporello format was used in the performative way during the final presentation.
